Smart City is to provide a web based platform to Varanasi to showcase all it has in terms of unique
businesses, places to see and things to do in the city.

Purpose
Smart City provides all the information of the city at one place. A user, whether he is a citizen or
tourist or a businessman can get all the relevant information. In addition the users can do business
remotely. The information has been structured distinctly according to end users. Conventionally a
user has to visit different sites for information about tourism, business and city news, remote
business required even greater efforts.
